◈ Signal-to-Execution Flow
Entry Confluence Filter: Quality gate that analyzes entry timing, price levels, and momentum alignment BEFORE execution. Prevents blind entries that turn winning signals into losers.
flowchart LR S1["Strategy Signal"] --> S2["Cognition Enhancement"] S2 --> Q1["Entry Confluence Filter"] Q1 --> Q2{"Quality?"} Q2 -->|PREMIUM/GOOD| R1["Risk Approval"] Q2 -->|MARGINAL| R2["Reduced Size"] Q2 -->|POOR| Q3["Queue for Better"] Q2 -->|REJECT| X["Rejected"] R1 --> E1["Execute"] R2 --> E1 Q3 -.->|price reached| Q1 style Q1 fill:#00d4ff,stroke:#00a8cc,color:#000 style X fill:#ff4444,stroke:#cc0000,color:#fff
Entry Confluence Scoring:
  • Level Score (30%): S/R, round numbers, EMAs, previous session levels
  • Momentum Score (20%): Bar momentum, RSI confirmation
  • Timing Score (25%): Range position, extension from levels
  • Structure Score (25%): Higher highs/lows alignment
