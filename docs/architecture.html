System Architecture

Overview of the 11-step trading pipeline and component responsibilities — concise and interactive.
01
Market Data Ingestion
Pull OHLCV data from MT5 with configurable timeframes.
02
Data Normalization
Transform raw feed into clean pandas DataFrames with timestamps.
03
Indicator Calculation
Compute SMA, ATR, RSI, MACD, Bollinger, Supertrend, VWAP efficiently.
04
Signal Generation
Strategies evaluate indicators and produce signals with confidence.
05
Risk Approval
Position sizing, exposure limits and daily loss checks are enforced.
06
Order Execution
Idempotent order placement with MT5 and fill verification.
07
Position Tracking
Real-time P&L tracking and age metrics for open positions.
08
Exit Detection
Priority-based evaluation of exit strategies (trailing, time, profit).
09
Position Close
Execute closes and persist trade records to the database.
10
Strategy Orchestrator
Dynamic strategy selection, arbitration, and safeties for multi-strategy runs.
11
Health Monitoring
Connection recovery, reconciliation, and metrics collection.
Data Layer
OHLCV normalization, multi-timeframe resampling, caching.
Indicators
Vectorized Supertrend, VWAP, RSI variants, ATR; strategy-driven compute.
Strategy Orchestrator
Regime detection, affinity mapping, dynamic switching with safeties.
